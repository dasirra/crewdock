04
Usage Guidelines
Do
Use the icon on dark or gradient backgrounds for maximum impact. The purple gradient is the primary expression of the brand.
Don't
Don't place the gradient icon on busy or colorful backgrounds. Use the monochrome white or dark variant instead.
Do
Maintain clear space around the icon equal to at least the height of the anchor ring. The icon should breathe.
Don't
Don't rotate, skew, or alter the proportions of the logo. Don't separate the claw marks from the anchor — they're a single unit.
Do
Use agent accent colors only for their designated agent. Overlord is always amber, Forge is always emerald, etc.
Don't
Don't use the agent accent colors as general-purpose brand colors. They exist only to identify their agent.
